The Fit2Live&Learn program is lucky to run its classes in Benson's recently modernized facilities. Modernization included historical preservation of the 1925 gymnasium building, as well as complete remodeling of the main gym, G-Wing classrooms, corridors and offices. Accessibility to Buckman field was also improved as part of the modernization.

Gymnasium
The new gym that has been patiently waited for has at last been completed. It is not only the newest gym but it is also the best gym in the city.
This new gymnasium is efficiently and modernly equipped to afford the student proper physical training. The gym floor proper is fifty by sixty feet and is made of solid maple. There is a balcony around the gym where an indoor running track is situated. This track can be used to hold spectators during basketball games. There is also sufficient room on the main floor to accommodate several hundred people. There is also some portable gym apparatus, and in the basement there are marble lined showers, lockers, store room, and a room made especially for drying clothing.
In the new building there are ten class rooms, four drawing rooms, one science laboratory and class room, and four academic rooms.
